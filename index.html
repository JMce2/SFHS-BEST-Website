Safety

Certification

  All students are required to complete a safety training course called Safety Pollution and prevention (SP2). SP2 is a company that provides a wide variety of safety training. Students are required to complete the Construction course in order to participate. In addition to many things, the course teaches kids about power tool safety, first aid, fire safety, and how to properly use personal protective equipment.

Programming

Robot C/Simulink

  When we coded, we began in RobotC because it was easier to understand for most people on the team, as many had worked with the language before. Simple things like the drivetrain motors (before speed control) were programmed from here and uploaded to the microcontroller so things like speed and torque could be tested and accounted for during the design process.
  Eventually, this was moved over to Simulink when the slightly more difficult things were programmed, as they could be simulated and confirmed to work before uploading to the robot.

Sketches

Design

  To analyze potential solutions, sketches and some rough cardboard models were made to see the idea form. While many people have an idea in their head, once it has been made into a 3D model it is easier to communicate your idea to others. It also forces the person who had to idea a recognize and address problems that they had not foreseen when thinking of it.
